The Position
The Data Acquisition Specialist (DAS) position is within Clinical Data Management. They work closely with clinical study teams and external vendors to define and develop the specifications and manage the acquisition of non-CRF data including IxRS, central laboratory (safety, PK, biomarkers), independent review facility and other data. Non-CRF data is growing in importance and complexity and the successful candidate will have the opportunity and challenge of learning about new data types and their relevance to clinical research in a company that is performing cutting-edge drug development.<?xml:namespace prefix = "o" ns = "urn:schemas-microsoft-com:office:office" />

 

Major Responsibilities and Accountabilities:

 

·         Provides data collection expertise to study teams as an extended team member and primary contact for data collection of non-CRF data.

·         Is responsible for the production of non-CRF data collection specification documentation.

·         Is responsible for the establishment of transfer accounts and for the receipt and delivery of non-CRF study data. 

·         Is responsible for conformance testing of incoming data files.

·         Develops and maintains effective working relationships with vendors and participates in vendor selection activities for a study in collaboration with study teams.

·         Applies data collection standards and understands their impact on downstream processes.

·         Collaborates with the Data Standards Office to define study data collection standards for new assessments and collaborates with them to implement new standards.

·         Represent CDM on IxRS sub teams and works with clinical study teams to develop IxRS specifications.

·         Participates in or leads process improvement and other initiatives.
