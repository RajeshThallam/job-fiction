JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Number: 01210261

Key Role: 
Provide support to Army clients by applying analytical and technical expertise to conduct acquisition project analysis, perform IT program requirements definition and development, propose solutions, and review technical products. Provide subject matter expertise to support deliverables, participate in designated meetings, and respond to queries from senior leadership, client staff, and military service stakeholders. Provide recommendations for revisions to existing documentation, including concept of operations (CONOPS) documents, functional requirements documents, system architecture design plans, engineering specification documents, service operations annexes, information technology service management (ITSM) service catalog, and service description documents. Provide support to program management staff responsible for defining and developing functional requirements and architecture documents in support of existing, planned, and other technological initiatives.
