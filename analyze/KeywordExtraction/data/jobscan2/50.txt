JOB DESCRIPTION
Job Number: 01209691

Key Role: 
Pre-author Technical Reference Model (TRM) entries and develop and maintain technical evaluation documentation, including document templates, training materials, and checklists used for conducting technical evaluation. Conduct independent research, analysis, and assessment of technology products, technologies, and technical standards as they relate to agency IT standards, IT environments, and reference architectures, as assigned. Identify process improvement opportunities within the TRM sites, including authoring, and report to team leadership. Participate in internal QA activities and provide guidance to other authors. Communicate findings to stakeholder users. Maintain responsibility for gathering, prioritizing, and documenting the analytical findings for the relevant technologies with the goal of facilitating a decision for final approval of a technology by the technical review board. Focus on technical reference model TRM activities as part of the larger enterprise architecture organization. Provide facilitation, analysis, documentation, presentation, and development of end-user communications and executive approvals.
