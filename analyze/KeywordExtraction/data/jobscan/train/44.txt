Job Description
 
Data Quality Analyst(Job Number: 00017544691)
 
Description
 
We are looking for a Data Quality Analyst. This is a client facing role & the candidate will have regular interactions with various client managers.
 
Responsibilities:
Analysis of the Data Stewardship activities along with SMEs
Proactive data quality profiling of the source systems & MDM Hub
Monitoring of data quality health
Analysis of data quality reports & presentation to the key stakeholders
Suggesting tactical & strategic DQ initiatives collectively with data governance team
Helping with analysis of downstream data quality issues
Tuning of data quality rules

Qualifications
 
Basic Qualifications:
5+ years of experience in Data Quality on in large/medium scale implementations
Working knowledge of Oracle, SQL server, and other major databases
Perform root cause analysis and resolution of issues with reports and data model
Excellent query writing skills
Good Team Player
Excellent communication skills and experience in onsite - offshore team coordination
