As an Antipiracy Services Business Intelligence Developer, you will own and expand Microsoft Operations proprietary piracy detection engine and platform, design and implement agile data processing algorithms, develop innovative reporting requirements, and will be equally at ease explaining what it all means to technical and non-technical counterparts.
,

,

Primary Job Functions include:
,

,

o Architecting, designing and implementing business intelligence tools that support massive datasets (Terabytes)
,

o Architect and design strategies and implantation to work with Big Data (both relational DBs and Stream Data).
,

o Partner with analysts and investigators to build agile algorithms designed to identify piracy.
,

o Partner with Channel Security Managers and antipiracy partners to drive and build solutions that meets their requirements, objectives and metrics
,

o Design Solutions for data mining and modeling, import massive datasets (Multi-Terabytes) that relate to vectors of the product key lifecycle to detect and identify suspect behavior.
,

o Design and Expand the (AAA) Agile Algorithm Augmentation for the existing Antipiracy Detection Engines to incorporate detection of new piracy Mos.
,

,
